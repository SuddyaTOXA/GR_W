Greyrock Fuel Products

  • Ultra Premium Diesel

    • High cetane ~ 70 (traditional Diesel 45)
    • Good lubricity ~ 400 (traditional Diesel > 520)
    • Reduces emissions
    • Can be used neat or as a premium blendstock

  • Zero Sulfur Kerosene

    • Reduces emissions over other kerosene products
    • Specialty applications due to virtually no sulfer

  • Gasoline Blendstock

    • No aromatics
    • Can be sold as C5+ or naptha/gasoline blendstock
    • Reasonable octane value at 60
    • Can be integrated with midsteam off-take or blended with oil

  • Purity Hydrogen

    • System can export hydrogen for use in industrial gas space
    • Co-location with hydrogen users possible
    • Emerging market for use of hydrogen as transportation fuel
